To Vee,

In a world of many faces,

Yours is a cherished light.

From the day we first connected,

You’ve made everything feel right.

Your laugh is like a bright sunrise,

That brightens up my day,

With every word and gesture,

You chase my doubts away.

Remembering when we first met,

Your kindness and your grace,

In every shared moment,

You’ve brought a smile to my face.

Each day that we’ve spent together,

Is a memory I hold dear,

Walking through life side by side,

With you, there’s nothing to fear.

So on this special day of yours,

I want to say with cheer,

That you are a wonderful friend,

Someone I’m grateful to have near.

Happy Birthday, Vee,

May your day be full of joy,

In the story of my life,

You’re a friend I truly enjoy.
